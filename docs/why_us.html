Why us

At Duff Tree Service we try really hard to provide quality pruning, safe removals, and trustworthy interactions. Check out some of the reasons you might want to choose a quality tree company below.

Careful cuts

In pruning operations for trees, the placement of cuts, and the cleanliness of the tools is important in preventing disease spread, mitigating damage, and producing the desired response, The right cut varies depending on whether you’re removing dead wood, removing branches over your house, or doing structural pruning to strengthen other branches.

Pruning trees when they are young is by far the most impactful and cost effective way to keep a tree healthy for many decades, in an environment where winds, and snow, and ice, are regular threats.

Safe Services

Logging and tree cutting are the most dangerous occupations in the country, and our arborists use the latest in PPE and tool technology to reduce the liability for the client and make sure everyone gets home safe to their family.

Many homeowners are unaware that if something bad happens and the company is not properly insured, the liability could be placed on them. When serious property damage, and human life are at risk, it’s always best to be able to fully trust that whoever you hire has the proper coverages. This includes general liability for ALL tree work (many landscapers policies only cover small trees), and workmans comp.

Informed Individuals

We place a high importance on continuous education, including daily talks as a crew, regular conferences, and certifications.

Tree work is a service that requires not only skill and physical ability, but also extensive knowledge of plant biology, pest and disease pressure, safety standards, pruning standards, and a working understanding of physics and loading forces.
